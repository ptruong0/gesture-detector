Instructions

Play Rock, Paper, Scissors against an AI opponent! First, start your camera and press Play. When the timer says "Shoot!", hold up either a fist (rock), an open hand (paper), or two pointed fingers (scissors).

Play Doubles: You can hold up two rocks, two papers, or two scissors instead of just one to earn double points if you win! Note that the opponent can also play doubles (unless this setting is disabled), so this increases the risk as well as the reward.

Note: Holding up two fingers will sometimes register as Scissors. Try holding up one finger instead since it is more likely to register.

Other Controls

Pinch to toggle autoplay on/off.

Hold up one open hand and one closed hand to allow/disable the opponent from playing doubles.

Swipe your open hand horizontally to reset the score.
